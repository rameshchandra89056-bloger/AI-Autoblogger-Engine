Privacy Policy

Aapki privacy hamare liye sabse zyada zaroori hai. Digital Kamai Hub par hum aapka data kaise use karte hain, uski jankari yahan di gayi hai:

  • Cookies: Hum website ka experience behtar banane aur Google AdSense ke ads dikhane ke liye cookies ka istemal karte hain.
  • Data Security: Hum aapki email ya personal jankari kisi third-party ko nahi bechte. Aapke Push Notifications ka data OneSignal ke secure server par rehta hai.
  • Third-Party Links: Hamari site par dusri websites ke links ho sakte hain. Un par click karne ke baad unki privacy policy laagu hogi.

Agar aapko koi sawal hai, toh aap humse sampark kar sakte hain.
